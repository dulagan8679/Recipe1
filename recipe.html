Adobo

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Bay Leaves
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat in soy sauce, garlic, and vinegar for at least an hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated meat and brown it on all sides.
  4. Pour in water, add bay leaves and black pepper, and let it simmer until tender.
  5. Reduce the sauce to your desired consistency, then serve with rice.

Pinakbet

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Onion, Garlic
  • Eggplant
  • Okra
  • Squash
  • Ampalaya

Steps

  1. In a pan, saute onion and garlic over medium heat until tender.
  2. Add the shrimp paste and continue to cook for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook for about 5 minutes or until softened.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil.
  5. Add more water in ½ cup increments as needed to maintain about 1 cup of liquid.
  6. Add squash and cook for 4 minutes.
  7. Add the shrimp paste and continue to cook for about 2 minutes.
  8. Add okra, long beans, Ampalaya, and Eggplant.
  9. Continue to cook for about 4 to 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender yet crisp.

Igado

Ingredients

  • Onion, Garlic
  • Bell Peppers
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Saute onions, bell peppers, potatoes and garlic in oil.
  2. Add tomatoes and pork strips, and stir fry until pork is light brown.
  3. Add in the liver and pour in the vinegar, soy sauce and the sugar. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer until the meat is cooked.
  5. Serve with steamed rice.
