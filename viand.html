Sinigang

Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bangus (Fish)
  • Long green pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Kangkong (Water Spinach)

Steps

  1. Boil fish in water with tomatoes for flavor.
  2. Once the fish is tender, add onions and other vegetables.
  3. Let the vegetables cook until tender, then serve the soup hot.

Kare-Kare

Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients

  • Oxtail
  • Peanut Butter
  • Chinese Cabbage (Pechay)
  • String Beans
  • Peanut Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil oxtail until tender.
  2. Prepare peanut sauce and add it to the boiled oxtail.
  3. Add pechay and string beans, then simmer until cooked.
  4. Serve with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side.

Tinola

Tinola Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chayote
  • Chinese Cabbage (Pechay)

Steps

  1. Brown chicken in oil.
  2. Add water and chayote, then simmer until chicken is cooked.
  3. Add pechay, cook for a few more minutes, and serve hot.

Pinakbet

Pinakbet Recipe

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Onion, Garlic
  • Eggplant
  • Okra
  • Squash
  • Ampalaya

Steps

  1. Sauté onions and garlic in oil.
  2. Add shrimp paste and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, water, and vegetables.
  4. Simmer until vegetables are tender yet crisp. Serve.
